Hi There!

Hello, I'm Linai...or at least that’s the internet handle I go by.

What do I do? As much as you (and I) may want to believe, I’m not a professional web surfer.

I’m not a designer (despite how much I’d like to be) nor am I a professional artist. In fact, I'm all self taught and haven’t even had any formal art training. Even though I won’t be submitting any resumes to Blizzard or Dreamworks (Okay, maybe I tried once or twice before...) I've been drawing since before I can remember. I enjoy putting around with Photoshop and reading about UI/UX; in fact, that’s how I got my start with this site.

I’m actually a software developer. Most of the time I don’t do anything flashy (I really hope to some day…) and sometimes some problems can be a real headache, but it's been a hell of a journey so far.

The Site

This site (formerly VRhapsody.com) has gone through several major revisions since the late 90's and has incorporated technologies such as PHP, MySQL, and Wordpress. In its current incarnation, it has been stripped down to the bare minimum; I've coded this entire site in VIM using HTML, CSS and Jekyll hosted on Github Pages.

The site is and probably will continue to be a work in progress. It is a place for me to experiment, observe, and interact with different kinds of concepts and technologies and I'm always changing and (hopefully) improving. After all, an artist's work is never truly done, is it?

If you're interested in changes and progress why not take a look at my github repository?
