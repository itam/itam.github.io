Art Usage Policy

  • Art may be used as long as proper credit is given to me and linked back to this site.
  • Art may be referenced as long as proper credit is given to me. This includes poses, tracings, backgrounds, etc. This DOES NOT include character designs or special world designs of any kind.
  • Art may not be used for profit of any kind.
  • Art may not be altered, cut, colored over, or mutilated in any way. This includes watermarks and signatures, do not remove them!
  • Art may not be claimed as your own work and/or uploaded to any site under your name.

Commissions Policy

  • Commissions will only be started once payment is confirmed.
  • Payments made via Paypal and Credit Card only.
  • All art may not be edited in any way, shape or form without permission after customer approval.
  • Rights to produce the images belongs only to me unless written and signed permission is given.
  • All image links will be invalidated a week after approval is given. Customer has until then to save and host resulting images independently. Image may be removed before deadline is reached if customer has approved of the final image(s).
  • Commissions will be finished within a month of payment date unless a prior arrangement has been agreed upon. Failure to deliver results in a 50% discount on the next commissioned image.
